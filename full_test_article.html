Ladli Behna Yojana is a major women empowerment scheme that provides direct benefit support to eligible women. If you want to check your payment status, complete eKYC, or understand eligibility conditions for 2026, this guide covers the full process step by step.

How to Check Ladli Behna Payment Status

  1. Visit the official scheme portal.
  2. Open the status or beneficiary section.
  3. Enter your registered mobile number or application ID.
  4. Submit details and review payment status, installment date, and account information.

Eligibility and Required Documents

  • Valid Aadhaar linked to a bank account
  • State residence proof
  • Income details as per current rules
  • Completed eKYC where required

Common Reasons for Payment Delay

  • Aadhaar-bank mismatch
  • Pending eKYC verification
  • Incorrect application details
  • Document validation under review

Frequently Asked Questions

How often is payment released?

Payment schedule depends on the latest official state notification for the scheme cycle.

Can I update bank details online?

Most portals allow correction requests through profile update or helpdesk workflows.

What if status shows pending?

Check eKYC, document status, and bank linkage first, then submit a grievance if required.
